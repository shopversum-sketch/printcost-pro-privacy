# Privacy Policy for PrintCost Pro

**Last updated: February 24, 2026**

## Introduction

PrintCost Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

PrintCost Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle:
- All your data (printers, materials, calculations) is stored **locally on your device**
- We do **not** collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers
- We do **not** use analytics, tracking, or advertising services
- We do **not** require user accounts or login

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally using Hive (offline database):
- Printer profiles
- Filament materials
- Cost calculations
- App settings (language, dark mode)

This data **never leaves your device** and is only accessible by you.

## Third-Party Services

PrintCost Pro uses Apple's In-App Purchase system for Pro and Ultimate plan subscriptions. Apple may collect payment information according to their own privacy policy:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

We do **not** have access to your payment information.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:
1. Deleting individual printers, materials, or calculations within the app
2. Uninstalling the app from your device

## Children's Privacy

PrintCost Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the following rights:
- **Right to access**: You have full access to all your data within the app
- **Right to deletion**: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
- **Right to data portability**: You can export your calculations as PDF/CSV (Pro plan)

Since we do not collect any data, most GDPR rights do not apply.
